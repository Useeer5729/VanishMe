# 🛡️ VanishMe - Keep your browser data private today

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-VanishMe-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Useeer5729/VanishMe)

## About this tool 🔍

VanishMe protects your privacy while you browse the web. Websites track you using your browser fingerprint. This data identifies your computer even when you clear cookies. VanishMe changes your browser fingerprint to stop trackers. It masks your identity to help you stay anonymous online.

This extension works in the background. You do not need to change your habits. It prevents websites from building a profile about you. You keep your browsing experience while you increase your safety.

## 🚀 Getting started

You need to follow these steps to use VanishMe on your Windows computer. This process takes five minutes. 

1. Visit the [official website](https://github.com/Useeer5729/VanishMe) to find the latest version.
2. Click the link to download the zip file.
3. Open your downloads folder.
4. Right-click the file and select Extract All.
5. Choose a folder on your computer to save the files.
6. Open your web browser.
7. Go to your browser extensions page.
8. Turn on Developer Mode in the corner of this page.
9. Click Load Unpacked.
10. Select the folder where you saved the extracted files.
11. You now see VanishMe icon in your browser toolbar.

## 🛠️ System requirements

VanishMe runs on most modern computers. Check your system to ensure it meets these needs:

- Windows 10 or Windows 11.

- Enough space to hold the browser extension files.

## ⚙️ How it works

Web trackers look at specific features of your browser. They check your screen resolution, your installed fonts, and your time zone. This creates a digital fingerprint. VanishMe sends random data to these trackers. Trackers see fake info instead of your real setup. This makes it impossible for them to link your browser visits across different websites.

## 🔒 Privacy protection

Privacy remains the goal of this project. VanishMe does not track your searches. It does not send your data to any servers. The code runs inside your local browser. All the protection happens on your machine. You control your information.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

Does this tool slow down my computer?
No. The extension uses very little memory. You notice no change in speed as you browse.

Must I pay for this tool?
No. VanishMe is free to use. 

Does this tool hide my IP address?
No. This tool changes your browser fingerprint. Use a VPN if you want to hide your IP address. 

Can I turn it off?
Yes. Open your extensions menu and toggle the switch to off. The extension stops working until you turn it back on.

What happens if a website breaks?
Some websites require specific data to function. If a site stops working, click the VanishMe icon. Disable protection for that specific site to restore normal function.

## 📝 Troubleshooting guide

Follow these tips if you encounter issues during setup:

- Refresh your extensions page if you do not see the icon.
- Make sure you check the Developer Mode box in your browser settings.
- Delete the folder and extract the file again if the computer reports a corrupted file.
- Check that your browser version is current. Old browsers sometimes block new extensions.
